Explore Sri Lanka's Regions

Colombo

Sri Lanka's vibrant capital where modernity meets colonial charm, with bustling markets and scenic beaches.

Kandy

Cultural heart of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and lush green hills.

Sigiriya

The ancient rock fortress known as the "Eighth Wonder of the World" with stunning frescoes.

Galle

Historic coastal city with a well-preserved Dutch fort and beautiful beaches.

Iconic Landmarks

Temple of the Tooth

Sacred Buddhist temple in Kandy housing the relic of the tooth of the Buddha.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient rock fortress and palace ruins with famous frescoes and lion's paw entrance.

Galle Fort

UNESCO-listed 17th-century Dutch fort with colonial architecture and ocean views.
